what_i_learned: |
  Today, our team focused on planning, scripting, organizing, and filming the elevator pitch for the DermaBridge project. We discussed the main points to include, such as the problem, our proposed solution, and the purpose of the project.

  We arranged the script in a clear order and removed unnecessary details. I learned that an elevator pitch should be short, direct, and easy for the audience to understand.

  After completing the script, we assigned speaking parts and filmed the presentation. Recording the pitch helped us practice speaking clearly, staying within the time limit, and presenting the project confidently.

blockers: |
  One challenge was deciding which information was important enough to include in the short pitch. We had several ideas, but the presentation needed to remain clear and within the time limit.

  Another challenge was making the script sound natural while filming. We also had to avoid repeating points, speak clearly, and make sure each team member's section connected smoothly.

reflection: |
  Today helped me understand the importance of planning and practicing before recording a presentation. A good elevator pitch needs to be organized, easy to follow, and focused on the main message.

  Filming the pitch also helped us notice areas where we needed to improve our delivery, timing, and transitions. By the end of the session, we had a completed recording and a clearer way of presenting DermaBridge.

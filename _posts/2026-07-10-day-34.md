what_i_learned: |
  Today, after encountering and troubleshooting many errors, we finally succeeded in running the DermaBridge application using an Android emulator on the computer.

  We tested different solutions, reviewed the error messages, corrected technical issues, and adjusted the development environment until the application began working properly. This experience helped us better understand how Android emulators can be used to test mobile applications without needing a physical Android device.

blockers: |
  Our main challenge was resolving the multiple errors that prevented the application from running. Some of the possible issues involved the app configuration, dependencies, file paths, emulator setup, and the connection between the development tools.

  The troubleshooting process required patience because fixing one error sometimes revealed another issue that also needed to be resolved.

reflection: |
  Successfully running the application was an important accomplishment for our team. Although the process involved many errors and repeated attempts, it showed us the importance of persistence and carefully reviewing technical problems.

  This experience improved our debugging and problem-solving skills and brought us closer to developing a functional DermaBridge mobile application.

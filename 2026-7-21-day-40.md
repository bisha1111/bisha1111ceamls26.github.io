what_i_learned: 
  Today, our team practiced and prepared our final pitch for the DermaBridge project. We reviewed the main points of our presentation and worked on clearly explaining the problem, our solution, and the purpose of our research.

  We also prepared and reviewed the presentation slides to make sure they were clear, organized, and matched what each team member would discuss during the pitch. Practicing together helped us improve our timing, confidence, transitions, and overall delivery.

blockers: 
  Our main challenge was deciding which information was most important to include in the pitch and on the slides. We also needed to make sure the presentation was not too long and that the slides supported our explanation without containing too much text.

reflection:
  Today’s practice helped us identify parts of the pitch and slides that needed improvement. Working together allowed us to make changes, organize our speaking sections, and become more comfortable presenting the DermaBridge project.

  Our team is now focused on completing the slides, improving the pitch, and preparing to present our work at the National Symposium on Effective and Ethical AI (N-SEA).
